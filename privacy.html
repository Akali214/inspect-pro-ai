Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Inspect Pro AI ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for functionality:

  • Authentication: We use Google Sign-In via Firebase to secure your account.
  • Cloud Sync: Inspection data and photos are stored securely on Firebase Cloud Storage/Firestore.
  • AI Processing: Report text is processed by Gemini AI to provide structured results. No data is used to train models without your consent.

2. Advertising

Our app uses AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads.

3. Permissions

The App requests Camera, Microphone (for voice input), and Storage permissions solely to perform inspection tasks.

4. Contact

For questions, contact the developer through our official distribution channels.
